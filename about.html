About Ying Chen, CPA

Ying Chen, CPA has earned a master degree in Accountancy from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and has more than 10 years of working experiences in public accounting, non-profit and industries specialized in taxation, small business accounting, and internal control procedures.
Ying Chen, CPA has over 10 years accounting and tax experience in numerous industries and different size companies, including some Fortune 500 company. I specialize in helping start-up companies, real estate agents and brokers, and small businesses. Of course, I also have many individuals as clients as well.
At Ying Chen, CPA, we don't just work with numbers; we work with people. Our clients are our most important asset, and helping them achieve and maintain success is our foremost goal. We strive for excellence by taking the time to fully understand our clients' needs and goals. We are highly knowledgeable and passionate about what we do, and the satisfaction of our clients is testament to that.
