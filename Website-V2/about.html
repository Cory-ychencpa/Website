About Us

Ying Chen, CPA earned a master degree in Accountancy from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and has more than 10 years of working experiences in public accounting, non-profit and industries specialized in taxation, small business accounting, and internal control procedures. At Ying Chen, CPA, we don't just work with numbers; we work with people. Our clients are our most important asset, and helping them achieve and maintain success is our foremost goal. We strive for excellence by taking the time to fully understand our clients' needs and goals. We are highly knowledgeable and passionate about what we do, and the satisfaction of our clients is testament to that.
